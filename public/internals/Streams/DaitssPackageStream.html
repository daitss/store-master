- (DaitssPackageStream) initialize(options = {})

The constructor produces a basic data stream; it returns key/value pairs of fixity information as known by DAITSS. The keys are sorted, unique URLs (strings) showing the storage-master provided location. The values are a datamapper-provided struct giving the expected ‘md5’, ‘sha1’, ‘ieid’ and ‘size’ of the package. ‘size’ is a fixnum. Two timestamps are provided as strings in UTC ‘Z’ format, namely last_successful_fixity_time and package_store_time.
